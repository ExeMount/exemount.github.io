About Me

Dynamic researcher with a strong academic background in Naval Architecture and Marine Engineering (PhD, MSc, BSc) and extensive international experience in high-fidelity CFD and multiphysics modelling. I have contributed to major EU Horizon2020 and Innovate UK programmes, applying advanced CFD simulation and digital-twin methods to complex fluid-mechanics problems. I was awarded the prestigious Global Talent Visa by the Royal Academy of Engineering in recognition of my contributions to the field of fluid mechanics.


I currently serve as Chief Technology Officer at Econavis Solutions, an R&D company developing sustainable propulsion and zero-emission marine technologies. Alongside this role, my academic background includes appointments as Research Associate at the University of Strathclyde, Lecturer and Research Assistant at Yildiz Technical University. Through projects such as the EU Horizon 2020 TrAM initiative and the Innovate UK Eco Boss Cap programme, I have contributed to the design of digital-twin frameworks and advanced propulsion concepts supporting the transition to net-zero transport.
